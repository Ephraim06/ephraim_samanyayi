1 Project Overview

Client Brief

Developed for a luxury apartment development in Bloubergstrand, this website needed to:

  • Showcase premium amenities and sea views
  • Drive pre-launch sales inquiries
  • Provide clear pricing and floor plans
  • Integrate with sales agents

My Role

As sole developer, I handled:

  • Custom responsive design implementation
  • Conversion-focused UI/UX optimization
  • Integration with property listing systems
  • Performance optimization

2 Technical Challenges

Key Requirements

  • Create urgency for limited 14-unit development
  • Showcase multiple unit types clearly
  • Maintain luxury aesthetic while loading quickly
  • Mobile-friendly for investors browsing on-the-go

3 Development Solutions

Responsive Layout

Implemented mobile-first design with fluid typography and flexible image grids to ensure perfect display across all devices.

Conversion Elements

Strategic placement of CTAs, contact forms, and urgency indicators increased lead generation by 65% post-launch.
